About OptiSched

OptiSched is an automated academic scheduling platform developed specifically for STI College Meycauayan. It leverages modern technology and artificial intelligence to eliminate the tedious manual process of creating conflict-free timetables for students, faculty, and institutional resources.

Mission: To streamline academic scheduling through intelligent automation, ensuring zero-conflict timetables while optimizing room utilization, faculty load balancing, and student experience at STI College Meycauayan.

Key Features

🧠

AI-Powered Scheduling

OptiBot, powered by advanced AI, resolves conflicts and optimizes schedules in real time.

🛡️

Constraint-Aware Engine

Respects hard constraints (no overlaps, room capacity) and soft preferences (teacher availability).

📱

Mobile-First Design

Built for a seamless experience on Android and iOS devices.

🔄

Real-Time Sync

Schedule changes propagate instantly across all users via real-time subscriptions.

👥

Role-Based Access

Dedicated dashboards for Administrators, Faculty, and Students with appropriate permissions.

📊

Visual Schedule Editor

Interactive weekly calendar view with drag-and-drop support and conflict highlighting.

How It Works

  1. Data Input: Administrators input subjects, teachers, rooms, sections, and scheduling constraints into the system.
  2. AI Optimization: The constraint-aware engine generates optimal timetables, automatically avoiding overlaps and respecting room capacities.
  3. Review & Publish: Administrators review the generated schedule, make manual adjustments if needed, and publish it.
  4. Real-Time Access: Students and faculty instantly see their personalized schedules in the mobile app.
  5. Ongoing Support: OptiBot provides 24/7 scheduling assistance, conflict detection, and data-driven insights.

For STI College Meycauayan

OptiSched was designed as a capstone research project to address the specific scheduling challenges faced by STI College Meycauayan. The system is tailored to handle the institution's programs, sections, room configurations, and faculty structures.
