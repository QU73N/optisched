Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how OptiSched ("we," "our," or "the Platform"), a scheduling application developed for STI College Meycauayan, collects, uses, and protects your personal information. By using OptiSched, you consent to the practices described herein.

1. Data Collection

OptiSched collects and processes the following categories of data in order to provide automated academic scheduling services:

👤 Account Information Full name, institutional email, role (admin/faculty/student), and authentication credentials.
📚 Academic Information Year level, program, section, department affiliation, and enrollment status.
📅 Scheduling Data Teaching preferences, availability, subject assignments, room assignments, and time slots.
🏫 Institutional Data Room capacities, equipment inventories, building/floor assignments, and enrollment counts.
📊 Usage Data Log data, session timestamps, and interactions with the OptiBot AI assistant.
💬 Communication Data Messages between users and administrators within the platform.

2. Purpose of Processing

🎯

Why We Process Your Data

  • Automated schedule generation and conflict resolution for academic timetabling.
  • Optimization of room utilization, faculty load balancing, and section placement.
  • Providing scheduling-related insights through the OptiBot AI assistant.
  • Account authentication and role-based access control.
  • System administration, troubleshooting, and performance monitoring.

3. AI Disclosure

OptiSched integrates Google's Gemini AI technology to power the OptiBot scheduling assistant. When you interact with OptiBot, your scheduling-related queries may be processed by Google's AI services. No personally identifiable information (PII) such as names, email addresses, or student IDs is sent to the AI provider. Only non-identifiable, anonymized scheduling data (e.g., time slots, room types, subject codes) is transmitted for processing.

4. Data Storage & Security

🔒

Security Measures

All data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database platform with enterprise-grade security:

🛡️ Row Level Security RLS policies restrict data access based on user roles.
🔐 Password Hashing Industry-standard bcrypt algorithms protect credentials.
🔗 Encrypted Transmission All data transmitted via HTTPS/TLS encryption.
🎫 JWT Authentication Token-based auth with automatic expiration.

5. User Rights

In accordance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173) of the Philippines:

📋
Right to Access Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
✏️
Right to Correct Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
🗑️
Right to Erasure Request deletion of your personal data, subject to lawful retention.
🚫
Right to Object Object to processing under certain circumstances.
📤
Right to Portability Request transfer of your data in a structured format.
📧
How to Exercise Contact the Data Privacy Officer through the Office of the Registrar.

6. Data Retention

Personal and scheduling data is retained for the duration of the academic term plus one (1) year for historical auditing and institutional record-keeping. After the retention period, data will be securely deleted or anonymized.

7. Third-Party Services

  • Vercel — Web Hosting
